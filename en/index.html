Japanese Festival: The Ultimate Cultural Celebration at Happy Wednesday Returns!

by The Hong Kong Jockey Club in partnership with the Consulate-General of Japan in Hong Kong and the Japan Racing Association. Experience a dazzling fusion of racing excitement and cultural immersion, including the Japan Racing Association Trophy on 5 November, alongside themed entertainment, cultural experience and authentic Japanese flavours. Mark your calendars: the festivities kick off with the day race on 2 November (Sun), followed by the night races on 5, 12 and 19 November.
