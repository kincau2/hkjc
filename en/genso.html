A new hotspot at Sha Tin Racecourse - Genso

“Genso” is a pioneering four-storey digital horse racing hub in Hong Kong with innovative phygitainment entertainment, Instagram-worthy experiences, and scrumptious dining.

The dining experiences span two floors. At 3/F FUDO TOWN Food Hall, it features five diverse food offerings from the Ramen Bar, Kanpai Circle, Don Express, Yoshoku Tei and Sui-tsu Corner. Marvel at the 1:1 horse-sized Digital Parade Ring, showcasing realtime live race and gamified information. Don’t miss the Interactive AI Horse Selection Stations with Race Simulator, designed to help customers make smart choices with comparable insights. At 4/F IZAKAYA, it provides a sit-down dining experience, offering both a set-menu and à la carte options.

At 3M/F “Genso Snap”, customers can explore the social engagement floor filled with Instagram-worthy experiences. Heading up to 4M/F, customers can immerse into phygitainment racing adventures by solving puzzles that uncovers racing historical stories along the tales of legendary horses at “Maze Race” — win the chance to enter the “Gensoverse” for a virtual jockey experience.

“Genso Isle” at 4/F showcases the horse-themed art-pieces, and a spectacular balcony where customers can watch world-class live racing.

Essential Tips for the Perfect Visit:
  • Reserve the dining experience at 3/F FUDO TOWN or 4/F IZAKAYA.
  • Pre-book Maze Race and Racing Academy sessions.
  • Use our Interactive AI Horse Selection Stations with Racing Simulator for AI horse analysis and Tipping One Racing Specialists’ top picks.
